GTUAcademy

A free, complete study portal built specifically for Gujarat Technological University engineering students.

Our Mission

GTUAcademy was created with one goal: give every GTU student access to the best possible study material — completely free, forever.

We believe quality education resources shouldn't sit behind paywalls. Every student deserves well-structured notes, solved PYQs, and clear explanations — regardless of their background or access to coaching.

Built by students, for students. Powered by a passion for making GTU exams less stressful and more approachable.
